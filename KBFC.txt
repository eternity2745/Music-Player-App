Kerala Blasters FC is a professional football club based in Kochi, Kerala, India. They compete in the Indian Super League, which is one of the top professional football leagues in India. The club was founded in 2014 and has since gained a large and passionate fan base. Kerala Blasters FC has a fierce rivalry with another Kerala-based club, ATK Mohun Bagan FC. The team plays its home matches at the Jawaharlal Nehru Stadium in Kochi. The club's colors are yellow and blue, and their mascot is a lion named Manjappada. Kerala Blasters FC has had some notable achievements, including finishing as runners-up in the inaugural season of the Indian Super League in 2014. They have a strong fan following and are known for their passionate support. The club has also been involved in various community and social initiatives, making a positive impact off the field as well.
