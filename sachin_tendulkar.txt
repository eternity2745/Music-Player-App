Sachin Tendulkar is a former Indian cricketer and one of the greatest batsmen in the history of cricket. He was born on April 24, 1973, in Mumbai, India. Tendulkar made his international debut in 1989 and went on to have a remarkable career spanning 24 years. He holds numerous records, including being the highest run-scorer in international cricket. Tendulkar is often referred to as the 'God of Cricket' and is considered a national hero in India.
